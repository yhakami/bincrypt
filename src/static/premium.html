Frequently Asked Questions

Why Bitcoin payments?
We use Bitcoin through BTCPay Server to maintain your privacy. No personal information, credit cards, or payment processors that could track or store your data.
Is Premium less secure than Free?
No! Premium uses the exact same zero-knowledge encryption as the free tier. We never have access to your unencrypted data, regardless of your plan.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can stop your subscription at any time. Your existing Premium pastes will continue to work until their expiration date.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your existing pastes remain accessible until their expiration date. New pastes will use the free tier limits (7 days, 512KB).
Do you store payment information?
No. All payments are processed through BTCPay Server and we don't store any payment or billing information.
Is there a free trial?
The free tier gives you a great sense of our service. Premium is only $5/month and you can cancel anytime if you're not satisfied.
