About BinCrypt

Zero-knowledge encrypted pastebin for secure text sharing

What is BinCrypt?

BinCrypt is a privacy-focused, zero-knowledge encrypted pastebin that allows you to share text securely without compromising your privacy. Unlike traditional pastebins, we never have access to your unencrypted data.

All encryption happens client-side in your browser using industry-standard cryptographic algorithms. Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device and can only be decrypted by someone with the encryption key.

We believe in privacy by design, transparency through open source, and building tools that put users in control of their data.

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Zero-knowledge means we literally cannot read your pastes, even if we wanted to. Here's how it works:

  • Client-side encryption: All encryption happens in your browser using Web Crypto API
  • AES-256-GCM: Military-grade encryption with authenticated encryption
  • PBKDF2 key derivation: 100,000 iterations with cryptographically secure salt
  • Secure random generation: Cryptographically secure random password generation
  • No key storage: Encryption keys never leave your browser or get stored on our servers

When you create a paste, your text is encrypted locally, and only the encrypted ciphertext is sent to our servers. The decryption key is embedded in the URL fragment (after the #), which is never sent to the server.

Privacy & Open Source

Privacy and transparency are core to our mission:

  • No tracking: We don't use analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts
  • Minimal data: We only store encrypted ciphertext and metadata
  • No logs: We don't log IP addresses or user activity
  • Open source: Full codebase available for audit and contribution
  • Self-hostable: Run your own instance with Docker

We believe that privacy tools should be transparent, auditable, and under user control. Our commitment to open source ensures that our privacy claims can be independently verified.
